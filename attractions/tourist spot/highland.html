Highland Mango Ecofarm

Step into Highland Ecofarm, Consolacion’s ultimate destination for nature lovers and adventurers. Unwind by a cozy campfire, spend the night under the stars with a peaceful camping experience, and take a refreshing dip in the farm’s serene natural pool. Nestled amidst rolling hills and vibrant greenery, Highland Ecofarm offers the perfect mix of adventure, relaxation, and breathtaking views—a getaway that will leave you in awe..

How to get there?

Purok Yellowbells, Brgy. Panoypoy, Consolasion

By Car

Navigate with Waze or Google Maps and set your destination to “Highland Mango Ecofarm” in Consolacion. Spacious parking is available.

By Commute

Take a jeepney with route number 62B heading to Pit-os and alight at Flying V Gasoline Station, which is the last stop for this route. From there, you can hire a habal-habal driver to take you to  Highland Mango Ecofarm. Fare is ₱100 to ₱150 per person.
