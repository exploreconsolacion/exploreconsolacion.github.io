How to get there?

Diversion Road, Tilhaong, Consolacion

By Car

Use Waze or Google Maps and set your destination to the Greenland Cabins, located along the road beside SM Consolacion, right after Jackbel Inn. The travel time from Cebu City is approximately 40 minutes, depending on traffic.

By Commute

From Cebu City, ride a jeepney or bus bound for Consolacion or northern Cebu. Get off at SM Consolacion, then hire a motorcycle (habal-habal) or taxi to take you to the Greenland Cabins.
