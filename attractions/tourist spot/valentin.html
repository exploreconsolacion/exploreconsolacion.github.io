Valentine Farm Resort

Valentin Farm Resort is a sprawling resort nestled in a lush mountainside valley of Consolacion, with three swimming pools adorned with exuberant vegetation and immaculately manicured grounds. This nature-filled resort is perfect for families and groups looking for a tropical mountain hideaway. I even witnessed a live proposal at Valentin during my first visit and I can totally claim that love was truly in the air.

How to get there?

Sitio Taya, Brgy. Polog, Consolacion, Philippines

By Car

Navigate with Google Maps or Waze then set the destination to “Valentin Farm Resort” in Consolacion. Parking space is available.

By Commute

From SM City Cebu, you can ride a jeepney going to Consolacion. If you’re from the Talamban/Pit-os area, there are also jeepneys that go to Consolacion.  You can drop off at Fooda Consolacion where afterwards you can ride a habal-habal  to take you to Valentin Farm. Average fair is around P50-P80 and make sure that you negotiate with the mentioned price.
