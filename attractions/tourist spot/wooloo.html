Things to do in Wooloo Resort

Hot Tub

Immerse yourself in the ultimate relaxation with Wooloo’s signature hot kawa bath. This traditional Filipino bathing experience involves soaking in a giant cauldron filled with warm water, herbs, and flowers. As you unwind, let the soothing heat relieve stress and muscle tension, offering a perfect retreat from your daily routine.

Relaxation

Enjoy a peaceful environment to relax and recharge, surrounded by nature and the perfect atmosphere for complete tranquility.

Spa

Indulge in a spa treatment that rejuvenates both body and mind, perfect for those seeking relaxation and healing.

How to get there?

Brgy. Garing, Consolacion, Cebu

By Car

To get to Wooloo Resort in Consolacion, drive along the North Coastal Road heading toward Barangay Garing. Use navigation apps like Waze or Google Maps with "Wooloo Resort" as your destination or the coordinates CWGR+M7J for precise directions. The road to the resort involves an uphill drive through a lush, mountainous area, so ensure your vehicle is in good condition for steep roads.

By Commute

From the North Bus Terminal near SM City Cebu, take a bus heading toward Consolacion and alight at Barangay Garing. From there, hire a local motorcycle (habal-habal) to take you to Wooloo Resort. The drivers are familiar with the area and can guide you safely through the uphill roads to the resort.
