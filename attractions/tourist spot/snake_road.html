Snake Road Consolacion

The "Snake Road" in Consolacion, Cebu, appears to be a popular mountain biking trail located in the Liloan/Lacion area. This trail is categorized as cross-country and is part of a network of six trails available for activities like hiking, trail running, and mountain biking. The region spans about 14 kilometers in total, with a descent of 842 meters and an elevation gain of up to 275 meters. Snake Road itself is well-known for its winding path, making it a favorite for bikers and adventure enthusiasts.

By Car

Use Waze or Google Maps and set your destination to Snake Road in Barangay Garing, Consolacion. From Cebu City, take Cebu North Road and head toward Consolacion Proper. Once in Consolacion, follow the signs or directions leading to Barangay Garing. From Cebu City, ride a jeepney or bus heading to Consolacion or northern Cebu and alight at the Consolacion public market. From there, hire a motorcycle (habal-habal) to take you to Snake Road. Inform the driver of your destination, as it is a well-known trail for outdoor activities.
