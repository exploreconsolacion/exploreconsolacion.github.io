San Narciso Parish Church

San Narciso Church, located in Consolacion, Cebu, is a historic parish known for its simple yet solemn atmosphere. It serves as a central place of worship for the local community and is a frequent choice for Sunday Mass and special ceremonies such as baptisms and weddings. The church features a traditional design that reflects the region's Catholic heritage, making it an appealing stop for tourists interested in cultural and religious landmarks.

How to get there?

Poblacion Occidental 6001 Consolacion

By Car

From Cebu City, take the Cebu North Road (also known as National Highway) heading toward Consolacion. The church is located near the town center, making it easily accessible. Look for local landmarks and signage as you approach the area.

By Commute
