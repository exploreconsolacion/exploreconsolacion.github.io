Nanda Café in Consolacion

Nestled in the serene hills of Consolacion, Nanda Café offers more than just great coffee—it’s an escape into nature. With panoramic views of lush mountains and towering trees, this cozy café is the perfect spot to relax and recharge. Whether you're savoring their signature brews or enjoying delectable pastries, every moment is accompanied by a refreshing breeze and a stunning backdrop. Ideal for quiet afternoons or catching up with friends, Nanda Café combines great flavors with a breathtaking ambiance, making it a must-visit destination in Consolacion!

Weekdays: 12NN to 9PM Weekends: 7AM to 9PM

By Commute

From Cebu City, ride a jeepney or bus bound for Consolacion (SM Consolacion area is a common landmark). Once in Consolacion, hire a habal-habal (motorbike taxi) to Nanda Café. Inform the driver that you’re headed to Nanda Café, which is a well-known spot for its mountain view and cozy ambiance.
