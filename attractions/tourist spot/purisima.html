How to get there?

Panoypoy, Consolacion, 6000 Cebu, Philippines

By Car

Drive along Cebu North Road and turn toward Barangay Garing. From there, follow the steep uphill route to Purisima Mountain View. Use Google Maps or Waze with the destination "Purisima Mountain View." Ensure your car is capable of handling sharp inclines.

By Commute

From the North Bus Terminal, take a bus heading to Consolacion. Get off at Barangay Garing and hire a habal-habal (motorcycle taxi). The driver will navigate the uphill climb to the viewpoint.
